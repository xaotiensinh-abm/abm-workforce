## Pro Tips: Infographic Styles
- Use specific hex colors for brand consistency
- Specify typography for cohesive design (Inter, Poppins, Montserrat)
- Include "professional" or "magazine quality" for premium results
- Glassmorphism and gradient effects work well for modern aesthetics
