## Pro Tips: Food & Product Photography
- Specify lighting setup (3-point, Rembrandt, side lighting)
- Include surface/backdrop materials (marble, slate, reclaimed wood)
- Add sensory details (steam, condensation, texture)
- Reference specific lens focal lengths (50mm, 85mm, macro)
- Name quality benchmarks (Michelin-star, magazine quality)
