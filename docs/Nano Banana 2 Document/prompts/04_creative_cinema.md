## Pro Tips: Creative Transformations & Cinema
- Use `--iw 1.6-1.8` for fantasy/sci-fi transformations
- Specify cinematic references (Marvel, Star Wars, Blade Runner)
- Include camera angles (low angle for power, eye level for connection)
- Add atmospheric elements (smoke, rain, particle effects)
- Quality benchmarks matter (Hollywood, blockbuster, CGI quality)
