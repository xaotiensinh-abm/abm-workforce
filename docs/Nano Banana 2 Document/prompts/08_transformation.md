## 💡 Pro Tips: Transformation & Motivation

1. **Split-screen/comparison prompts need strong visual contrast**
   - Lighting: "dimly lit room" vs "bright gym"
   - Color: "black and white" vs "vivid color"
   - Environment: "cluttered" vs "organized"

2. **Be specific about timeline details**
   - "BEFORE: JANUARY 2023" vs "AFTER: JANUARY 2025" adds authenticity
   - Include progress markers (dates, milestones)

3. **Body language tells the story**
   - "slouched, defeated" vs "standing tall, confident"
   - "protective body language" vs "open, welcoming"

4. **Environmental contrast reinforces transformation**
   - "dark bedroom, closed curtains" vs "outdoors in nature, sunshine"
   - "sleeping in car" vs "luxury penthouse"

5. **For fitness transformations, mention specific details**
   - "baggy clothes" vs "fitted athletic wear"
   - "pale" vs "tan and healthy"
   - "poor posture" vs "confident pose"
