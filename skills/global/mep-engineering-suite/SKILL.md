## Step 2: Route đến Engine phù hợp

### DESIGN Mode:
1. Load skill `mep-design-automation` (tại `~/.gemini/antigravity/skills/mep-design-automation/SKILL.md`)
2. Follow Phase 0→1→2→3 của design skill

### TENDER Mode:
1. Load skill `mep-tender-automation` (tại `~/.gemini/antigravity/skills/mep-tender-automation/SKILL.md`)
2. Follow Phase 1→6 của tender skill

### COMBINED Mode (Design → Tender):
1. Chạy DESIGN Mode trước → sinh Equipment List + BoQ draft
2. Feed design output vào TENDER Mode:
   - Equipment quantities từ design → BoQ item counts
   - Calc sheets → verify technical specs in TD
   - Equipment List → cross-check tender BoQ
3. Chạy TENDER Mode với data đã enrich từ design

## Step 3: Cross-Reference Intelligence

| BoQ draft (quantities) | BoQ-PRICED (with prices) | Items complete? |

## Step 4: Output Delivery

Tổng hợp output theo mode:
- **DESIGN**: 11 Excel files + DESIGN-BRIEF.md

- **COMBINED**: All design + tender files + cross-reference report
