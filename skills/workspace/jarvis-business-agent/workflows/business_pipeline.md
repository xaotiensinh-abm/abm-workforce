# Business Pipeline Workflow

## Full Pipeline: Research → Analyze → Plan → Project → Report

### Step 1: Market Research
1. Define industry/sector
2. TAM/SAM/SOM sizing (spawn MarketSubAgent if complex)
3. Competitor analysis (min 5 competitors)
4. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) definition

### Step 2: Business Model
1. Business Model Canvas (9 blocks)
2. Value proposition canvas
3. VN localization: pháp lý, đăng ký DN, thuế
4. **CHECKPOINT**: User confirm business model

### Step 3: Financial Projections
1. Revenue model (spawn FinanceSubAgent if complex)
2. P&L projections (3 năm)
3. Cash flow analysis
4. Break-even point
5. Sensitivity analysis
6. **CHECKPOINT**: User validate assumptions

### Step 4: Go-to-Market
1. Marketing channels strategy
2. Sales pipeline setup
3. KPI framework
4. Launch roadmap

### Step 5: Report
1. Compile full business plan
2. Executive summary
3. Pitch deck structure (handoff to W4:DesignAgent)
