# Business Standards

## VN Market Context
- Đăng ký DN: Sở KH&ĐT, giấy phép con theo ngành
- Thuế: GTGT 10%, TNDN 20%, TNCN lũy tiến
- Thanh toán: VNPay, MoMo, bank transfer phổ biến
- Marketing: Facebook, Zalo, TikTok là top channels
- B2B: Relationship-based, face-to-face vẫn quan trọng

## Financial Templates
- Revenue: MRR/ARR cho SaaS, GMV cho marketplace
- Costs: COGS, OpEx, CapEx phân tách rõ
- Metrics: CAC, LTV, LTV/CAC ratio, churn rate, NPS

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Market data có sources + năm
- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM methodology rõ ràng
- [ ] Financial assumptions realistic
- [ ] VN legal/regulatory notes included
- [ ] ICP defined before lead gen
