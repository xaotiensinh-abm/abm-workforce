# Marketing Pipeline
## Flow: Idea → Draft → Review → Publish → Track

### Stage 1: Idea (marketing-specialist)
- Input: Business goal / campaign brief
- Output: Content brief + keyword target
- Skills: content-strategy, seo-fundamentals

### Stage 2: Draft (content-writer worker)
- Input: Content brief
- Output: Draft content (blog/social/email)
- Skills: content-creator, copywriting

### Stage 3: Review (Jarvis)
- Input: Draft
- Check: Brand voice, SEO, Vietnamese quality
- Decision: Approve / Revise / Reject

### Stage 4: Publish
- Input: Approved content
- Output: Published to platform
- Track: Engagement metrics after 48h

### Stage 5: Optimize
- Input: Performance data (7 days)
- Action: A/B test, adjust, repurpose
