# Competitive Landscape — ABM vs Competitors

> Updated: 2026-03-14

## Direct Competitors (VN AI Coaching)
| Competitor | Price | Duration | Model | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|:-----:|:--------:|-------|----------|----------|
| ABM Workforce | 250tr | 12 months | 1-1 Done-With-You | Structured, SEAL team | New brand |
| Generic AI trainers | 5-50tr | 1-5 days | Workshop | Cheap, quick | No follow-up |
| International coaches | 500tr+ | 6-12m | 1-1 | Brand, network | Not Vietnamese |
| Self-study courses | 1-10tr | Self-paced | Video | Cheap, flexible | No support |

## ABM USPs (Unique Selling Points)
1. ONLY 1-1 coaching 12 tháng bằng tiếng Việt + AI cho SME
2. SEAL team 8 người — không chỉ 1 coach
3. Brain-First (tư duy trước, tool sau)
4. Done-With-You (khách là pilot, ABM là architect)
5. 4 deliverables: Knowledge Base, Portal, Chatbot, Workflows

## Indirect Competitors
- McKinsey/BCG consulting: quá đắt (>1 tỷ)
- Freelance AI consultants: không structured
- AI tool vendors (ChatGPT Plus): self-service, no coaching
- MBA programs: theory, not AI-practical

## Market Positioning
Premium tier, niche: AI transformation coaching cho SME CEOs VN
Not competing on price → competing on VALUE + OUTCOME
