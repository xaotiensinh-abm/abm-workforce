# Training Content — Quality Checklist

## Content Quality Gates
- [ ] Target audience clearly defined
- [ ] Prerequisites stated
- [ ] Learning path progressive (basic > intermediate > advanced)
- [ ] Real-world examples included
- [ ] Practice exercises provided
- [ ] Assessment questions written
- [ ] Estimated completion time accurate

## Output Format Standards
- Slide deck: max 50 slides/module
- Video lesson: max 15 min/video
- Written guide: max 2000 words/chapter
- Quiz: min 5 questions/module
