# Veo Video Gen — Output Specifications

## Supported Outputs
- Resolution: 720p, 1080p, 4K
- Duration: 4s clips (combinable)
- Aspect: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1
- FPS: 24, 30

## Example Prompts
- Product demo: "Smooth camera orbit around [product] on white table..."
- Social video: "Dynamic text animation introducing coaching program..."
- Avatar: "Professional mentor speaking to camera, warm lighting..."

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Prompt includes camera movement
- [ ] Lighting described
- [ ] Subject clearly defined
- [ ] Duration specified
- [ ] Resolution appropriate for platform
