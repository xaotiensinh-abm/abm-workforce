description: Tìm kiếm và qualify leads cho sản phẩm/dịch vụ
---

Workflow tìm kiếm leads chất lượng cao: phân tích business, tìm target companies, cung cấp contact strategies.

---

## Khi nào dùng

- Tìm potential customers cho product/service
- Xây dựng danh sách companies để partnership
- Identify target accounts cho sales outreach
- Chuẩn bị business development activities

---

## Workflow

### 1. Hiểu Product/Service

Nếu ở trong code directory → analyze codebase để hiểu product

Hỏi:
- Value proposition là gì?
- Key features và benefits?
- Giải quyết vấn đề gì?

### 2. Define Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- **Industry/Sector**: Tech, Fintech, Healthcare, etc.
- **Company size**: Startup, SME, Enterprise
- **Location**: Vietnam, SEA, Global
- **Pain points**: Relevant problems
- **Technology**: Tech stack requirements

### 3. Research & Identify Leads

Tìm signals:
- Job postings liên quan
- Technology stack
- Recent news (funding, expansion)
- Complementary products/services

### 4. Score & Prioritize

Fit Score (1-10) based on:
- ICP alignment
- Immediate need signals
- Budget availability
- Timing indicators
